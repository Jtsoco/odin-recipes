There's nothing quite like coming home on a chilly fall day to a plate of some warm and savory treat to heat up both your body and your heart. With this recipe you'll have a delicious batch of fresh, warm brownies ready for when your kids or spouse get home, and no matter how much the weather gets them down they'll be given a smile to end the day guaranteed. In this recipe we'll show you how to prepare box brownies to perfection, and even offer ideas on how to throw a twist into the traditional methods!

Ingredients

  • Brownie Box Mix
  • Eggs 2
  • Milk 3 Tablespoons
  • Vegetable Oil 1/2 cup
  • That's it! Easy, right?

Cooking!

  1. Heat oven to 170 Celsius
  2. Stir brownie mix, milk, oil, and eggs in a medium bowl until well blended.
  3. Grease a 9x9 pan (Butter is okay to use).
  4. Pour into pan, bake for 25 minutes.

Add Variety!

  • Throw candy in! M&Ms, Reeses, even some crushed Oreos!
  • Nuts are a great way to add flavor. Consider peanuts, almonds, even walnuts!
  • Sprinkles can add some fun color to any brownie!
  • Throw in some cookie dough chunks for a special cookie brownie hybrid!
