What more could you want than pizza? Keeps you warm, keeps you filled, keeps you happy! Unlike most online-recipes I won't serenade you with the story of that time a pizza saved my fathers life, because I don't need to justify pizza. It's pizza, you want it, I want it, and the guy who robbed my dad wanted it. Let's get straight to it.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Pizza bread
  • Mixed shredded cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Literally any vegetables and toppings you want, just make sure to chop them first.

Spices

  • Literally anything you want. If you don't know, here's an idea.
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Chili Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat oven to 225 Celsius
  2. Brush top of bread with olive oil
  3. Mix spices with tomato sauce.
  4. Add chopped toppings
  5. Put in oven until crust is browned and cheese golden. About 10-15 minutes.
  6. Success! Enjoy.
