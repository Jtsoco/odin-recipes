Odin Pork Tacos

Home

The best home-made tacos around!

A simple, easy, and delicious recipe to make tacos for the whole family to love. It takes very little time to make quite a lot, and will leave your whole family smiling. Watch a netflix show as you cook, or have something easy to make with the kids, either way its a stress-free easy recipe!

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork
  • Taco Shells
  • Onions
  • Peppers (your choice!)
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese (Your choice)

Spices

  • Smoked Paprika
  • Chili Powder
  • Oregano
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Cook the ground pork, chopping it as you cook.
  2. Mix tomato sauce with spices to taste.
  3. When the pork changes color, drain the grease and add the tomato sauce.
  4. While you periodically stir the meat, chop the vegetables.
  5. Heat the taco shells in the oven for 10 min at 100 celsius.
  6. Put whatever vegetables you like, the meat, and the cheese in the taco. Enjoy!
